##### 10. How could we improve or alter this familiarisation exercise?

A more straightforward question pattern would be really challenging and would help us understand how an engineer approaches a problem.

For example, providing a sample link or a error log output to the engineer along with screenshots to the issue faced during deployment could really kindle the creative part to actively seek solutions to very particular issues. 

Right now the questions have a wide scope, narrowing down to specific problems would give us a closer look into how someone approaches the problem.
